About

My name is Monika Mrozek. This is a short overview of my work.

I'm an architect. After my study I worked mainly for companies which occupation is facades design. That was pretty interesting and I gained a lot of technical knowledge at that time. But for now I think, I would be more satisfied and useful doing something more creative, so I can train my other design skills.

Recently I moved back from Oslo to Wrocław. You can find some of my norwegian stories on my blog. In my spare time I cook, draw or try to convince some people to let me cut their hair.

Work

All those drawings are parts of executive designs of public utility buildings. Some of them are drawn by me from the start and I was effectively participating in design process, which was pretty cool experience. But apart from that, I use to work as a simple draftsman, who drew plenty of lines and asked superiors about purpose of almost every screw. Fortunately they were incredibly patient, so anyway I learned a lot about facades design and a number of new German words.
